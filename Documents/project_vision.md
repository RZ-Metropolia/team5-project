# Product Vision: Homework Submission and Tracking System

## Problem Statement
Students and teachers struggle of to efficiently manage homeworks using paper or email. Papers get lost, and students often don't know their grades or feedback until it is too late.

## Target Audience
Our product is designed for educational institutions, specifically:
- Students who need a clear way to submit homework and get feedbacks from teachers.
- Teachers who require an organized method to collect, grade, and provide feedback on assignments.

## Value Proposition
Our product provides users with a easy-to-use and fluent digital interface that ensures data integrity and ease of access. Unlike paper or email, our system makes sure home work is never lost and helps teachers grade faster.

## Key Features and Functionality
- **Homework Uploads**: Students can easily upload their homeworks and check if the teacher received them.
- **Grading Tools**: Teachers can view the homework, give a grade, and write comments.
- **Deadline Alerts**: The app notifies students when a due date is coming up so they don't forget.
- **Interactive UI**: A responsive desktop interface for both submission and grading systems.

## Goals and Objectives
- Get 100% of the classes to use the system.
- Cut the time teachers spend organizing papers by half within the first semester.
- Improve student satisfaction rate regarding deadline and feedback notification systems to 95%.

## Vision Statement
Our vision is to make handing in and grading homework stress-free and paperless. We aim to simplify the boring homework organization tasks so that students can focus on studying and teachers on teaching.
